About

Welcome to ClimbingDreamStudious, your number one source for all things climbing related. We're dedicated to providing you the very best experience, with an emphasis on detail. Founded in 2000 by Liam Stage, ClimbingDreamStudious has come a long way from its beginnings in Smitch Rock. When Liam first started out, his passion for climbing and photography drove him to start his own business. We hope you enjoy our services as much as we enjoy offering them to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don't hesitate to contact us.
